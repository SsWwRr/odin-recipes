Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs, but scrambled

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 5 eggs
  • pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Break the eggs and put them in a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of pepper and salt
  3. Mix the ingredients together
  4. Pour the ingredients onto a pan
  5. Cook on medium heat till the desired degree of doneness
